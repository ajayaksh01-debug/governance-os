# Ethana Customer Claims Matrix

This claims matrix defines the boundaries between marketing positioning and operational reality for the Ethana platform. All customer-facing claims, proposal wording, and sales interactions must adhere to these audited statuses, approved claims, and selling rules.

---

## 1. Ethana Edge — Endpoint AI Monitoring & Governance

| Product Capability | Audited Status | Approved Customer-Facing Claim | Technical Reality / Limitation | Approved Selling Rule | Regulatory Framework Alignment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Device-Level AI Discovery & Inventory** | **Beta** | "Instantly build a comprehensive inventory of all AI tools, browser extensions, developer assistants, and local models active across the organization." | Endpoint agent-based discovery only. Connectors to Okta and SaaS provider APIs (IdP connectors) are **In Build**. Does not track personal devices on personal networks. | **Position as Beta.** Pitch only as a controlled proof-of-concept. State that automated out-of-band SaaS/IdP discovery is currently In Build. | **RBI Digital Risk Guidelines**, **IRDAI Cybersecurity Guidelines**, **SEBI**, **NIST AI RMF (MAP)** |
| **Browser prompt & response monitoring** | **Beta** | "Capture an audit trail of employee prompts and responses across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other consumer AI surfaces to prevent data loss." | Observability only. Egress blocking and active browser redaction are **Roadmap** capabilities. Content is logged but not stopped before it leaves the browser. | **Position as Beta.** Frame as a diagnostic observability tool rather than a real-time firewall or DLP engine. | **DPDP Act (Accountability)**, **GDPR Art. 5 (Accountability)**, **FCA Consumer Duty** |
| **Developer AI Tool Monitoring** | **Beta** | "Audit the prompt history, source code sharing, and tool configuration of developers using Cursor, Copilot, Claude Code, and other IDE assistants." | Observability only. Active blocking or source-code masking before API transit is **Roadmap**. | **Position as Beta.** Highlight in IT Services for developer auditing, but clarify that real-time source code redaction is roadmap. | **Client MSA Security Audits**, **ISO 27001 (A.12.4 Logging)** |
| **Per-User Attribution** | **In Build / Roadmap** | "Attribute every endpoint AI session and prompt to a specific active employee identity." | Tracks endpoint identity. Full enterprise-wide FinOps user/team cost breakdown is **In Build**. | **Do not claim as GA.** Pitch as an In-Build capability that is part of the upcoming roadmap. | **GDPR (Accountability)**, **DPDP Act** |
| **Edge-level PII & Secrets Redaction** | **Roadmap** | "Redact PII, secrets, and source code on the device before prompts leave the browser." | **Roadmap / Not available.** Redaction today is available only on the **Ethana Build Gateway**, not at the endpoint/browser level. | **Do not claim availability.** Present strictly as a future roadmap item. Sell Cursory assessments to design endpoint policies in the interim. | **DPDP Act**, **GDPR Art. 5**, **HIPAA** |
| **AI Website Egress Blocking** | **Roadmap** | "Enforce blocklists and coaching redirects on unauthorized AI websites at the browser layer." | **Roadmap / Not available.** Blocking and redirect capabilities are currently in development. | **Do not claim availability.** Sell the Discovery value first; blocking is the future policy enforcement layer. | **RBI IT Outsourcing**, **IRDAI Guidelines** |

## 2. Ethana Workspace — Governed AI Workspaces & Knowledge Assistants

> [!CAUTION]
> **Ethana Workspace is entirely unverified in product engineering files.** All capabilities listed below must be withheld from commercial proposals until engineering has validated the codebase.

| Product Capability | Audited Status | Approved Customer-Facing Claim | Technical Reality / Limitation | Approved Selling Rule | Regulatory Framework Alignment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Governed Enterprise Chat** | **Unverified – Product Validation Required** | "Provide employees with a self-hosted, VPC-deployed AI chat workspace where data and chats never leave your controlled network." | The chat wrapper is fully hosted. However, model responses depend on the API endpoint routed to (SaaS models vs. self-hosted models). | **Do not claim or sell.** Withhold from active proposals. Explain to prospects that Workspace is undergoing engineering validation. | **GDPR (Data Residency)**, **DPDP Act (Data Localisation)** |
| **Knowledge-Grounded RAG Assistants** | **Unverified – Product Validation Required** | "Deploy department copilots that query SharePoint, Confluence, Notion, and databases with role-based access control." | Ethana governs the LLM interaction layer. It **cannot** audit or fix outdated, biased, or incorrect documents inside the vector database or document sources. | **Do not claim or sell.** Withhold from active proposals. RAG document lifecycle management requires a separate CMS governance process. | **FCA Consumer Duty (Outcome testing)**, **ISO 42001 Cl.8** |
| **PII Auto-Masking in Storage** | **Unverified – Product Validation Required** | "Auto-mask PII inside ingested documents and vector storage, unmasking it only for authorized roles." | Only applies to files ingested through the Workspace pipeline. Does not govern external corporate databases or raw files. | **Do not claim or sell.** Withhold from active proposals. | **DPDP Act (Data Minimisation)**, **GDPR Art. 5** |

## 3. Ethana Build — Enterprise AI Infrastructure & Control Plane

| Product Capability | Audited Status | Approved Customer-Facing Claim | Technical Reality / Limitation | Approved Selling Rule | Regulatory Framework Alignment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **LLM Gateway & Routing** | **Production (GA)** | "A unified, multi-model API front door supporting routing, fallback, and cost-captures with ~50ms latency." | Gateway is LLM-only. Proxying and logging non-LLM REST APIs (AVM, NLP classifiers, ASR) is **Unconfirmed / Out of Scope**. | **Lead with infrastructure.** Position as vendor-neutral infrastructure that prevents model lock-in. | **ISO 42001 Cl.8**, **RBI IT Outsourcing** |
| **Runtime Guardrails** | **Production (GA)** | "Bidirectional inline scanners checking prompts and responses for PII, prompt injection, secrets, jailbreaks, toxicity, and bias." | The Bias scanner is a runtime text filter; it is **not** a model bias audit. | **Immutable Audit Logs** | **Production (GA)** | "Capture a write-once, tamper-proof audit trail of every AI interaction with native forwarding to your SIEM (Splunk, Elastic, Datadog)." | Logs only traffic routed through the gateway. Bypassed calls are unlogged. Customizing the schema to specific regulator fields requires a configuration engagement. | **Lead in all regulated conversations.** This is Ethana's clearest, most defensible, and most valuable enterprise capability. | **EU AI Act Art. 12 (Record-keeping)**, **FCA SYSC 9 (7-year retention)**, **RBI IT Outsourcing**, **ISO 42001 Cl.9.1** |
| **MCP Security Broker** | **Production (GA)** | "Register, authorize, rate-limit, and audit tool and database calls made by autonomous agents." | The core broker works. | **Visual Agent Builder** | **Unverified – Product Validation Required** | "Drag-and-drop DAG workflow design with agents, APIs, loops, evaluators, and human-in-the-loop steps." | No codebase or engineering documentation exists to support the presence of a visual builder. | **Do not claim or sell.** Withhold from active proposals. | **N/A** |
| **Red Teaming Orchestrator** | **Production (GA)** | "Simulate automated adversarial attacks against models, applications, and agents using 21 built-in OWASP probes." | Proactive testing only. CI/CD integration is **In Build**. | **Deepen security conversations.** Differentiate from runtime guardrails. Do not claim the CI/CD pull-request gate is live. | **EU AI Act Art. 15 (Robustness)**, **NIST AI RMF (MEASURE)**, **OWASP LLM Top 10** |
| **Cost Controls** | **Production (GA)** | "Set real-time project budget caps and alerts with virtual API keys per team." | Project-level cost tracking is live. | **Compliance Pack & Evidence Export** | **In Build** | "One-click export of structured compliance evidence for EU AI Act, ISO 42001, and NIST AI RMF." | **In Build / Not available.** Automated evidence collection is not live. Evidence collection today must be delivered via Cursory Services. | **Do not claim availability.** Sell the underlying Immutable Audit Log as the evidence source and Cursory Services as the delivery mechanism. | **EU AI Act Art. 11**, **ISO 42001 Cl.9.2**, **NIST AI RMF** |
