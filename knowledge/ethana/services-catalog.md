# Cursory Services — Catalog

Five service lines. Each is human-delivered. None carries a platform status flag. Indicative durations are included for procurement scoping; pricing is held in the separate pricing document and is not duplicated here to avoid drift.

---

## 1. AI Governance Assessments

Point-in-time evaluation of a client's AI estate and governance posture.

| Engagement | What it delivers | Indicative duration |
|---|---|---|
| AI Governance Readiness Assessment | Audit of the current AI estate, gap analysis against ISO 42001 / NIST AI RMF / EU AI Act / RBI FREE-AI, risk register, prioritised remediation roadmap. | 4 to 6 weeks |
| AI Inventory and Classification | Full AI asset register mapped to regulatory exposure, sanctioned versus shadow, risk scoring per system. | 3 to 4 weeks |
| AI Governance Health Check | Annual maturity assessment against ISO 42001, evidence-collection support for auditors, compliance posture scorecard. | Annual |

Platform relationship: the AI Inventory engagement is the human-delivered version of the platform's Discovery capability, which is Roadmap. Deliver the inventory as a service now; do not imply the platform produces it.

---

## 2. Framework Mapping

Mapping a client's AI systems and controls to a specific regulatory framework, with a gap list and remediation priorities.

| Engagement | What it delivers | Indicative duration |
|---|---|---|
| Regulatory Gap Analysis (per framework) | Control mapping, gap list, and remediation priorities for one framework: RBI FREE-AI, EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, or another in scope. | 2 to 3 weeks per framework |

Platform relationship: the platform's Compliance Pack (automated evidence collectors and one-click export across EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, MITRE ATLAS) is In Build. Until it ships, this mapping is consultant-delivered. See `../framework-crosswalk.md` for the underlying capability-to-framework map.

## 3. Regulatory Analysis

Ongoing intelligence on the changing AI regulatory landscape, translated into specific control implications for the client.

| Engagement | What it delivers | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Regulatory Horizon Monitoring | Briefing on RBI / IRDAI / SEBI / EU AI Act / DPDP developments, with the control implications for the client and a board-ready summary. | Monthly |

Note: this replaces the "Regulatory Intelligence and Watch" capability that the earlier files incorrectly listed as a platform feature. It is a service. The platform does not monitor regulation.

## 4. Governance Programs

Building and running the client's AI governance capability: policy, controls, implementation, and ongoing operation.

| Engagement | What it delivers | Cadence / duration |
|---|---|---|
| AI Policy and Control Framework Design | AI Acceptable Use Policy, AI Risk Appetite Statement, AI Governance Framework document, AI Control Library. | 3 to 5 weeks |
| Ethana Implementation | Gateway configuration, guardrail policy development, audit-log schema design, SIEM integration, user training. | 4 to 8 weeks |
| Managed AI Governance Retainer | Ongoing guardrail policy updates, quarterly risk reviews, regulatory change monitoring, governance committee support, escalation advisory. | Monthly / quarterly |
| Managed Ethana Operations | Platform monitoring, guardrail tuning, alert triage, model cost optimisation, quarterly reporting to the CISO. | Ongoing |

Platform relationship: Ethana Implementation and Managed Ethana Operations configure and run the platform. State the platform capability (status-flagged) and the human delivery separately.

---

## 5. Advisory Services

Specialist advisory and testing delivered as expert services rather than as standing programs.

| Engagement | What it delivers | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| AI Red Teaming as a Service | Quarterly red-team exercises on production AI systems, custom probe development for bank-specific attack patterns, findings-to-policy remediation. | Quarterly |
| Control implementation advisory | Prioritised control recommendations, implementation playbooks, technical architecture guidance, tooling recommendations. | Project-based |
| Escalation and governance-committee advisory | Expert support to the client's AI governance committee and on live escalations. | On demand |

Platform relationship: Red Teaming as a Service uses the platform's red-teaming orchestrator (21 OWASP probes, Production). The custom probe development and remediation are the human-delivered IP.
